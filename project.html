Hoan Kiem Lake

It is a quiet lake in central Hanoi that is ideal for walking and sightseeing, and where citizens can rest. On the promenade around the lake, you can relax on benches, see portraits, and enjoy walking around by electric cars and cyclos.

Thang Long Water Puppet Theatre

It is a puppet show introduced in rural Vietnam and is a traditional performance characterized by comical movements using hidden devices. The theme of the play incorporates rural life and lessons and is played along with traditional instruments and folk songs.

The language is Vietnamese, but even if you don't understand the meaning, you can fully enjoy the doll's movements. The theater is located near Hoan Kiem Lake and was built by Ho Chi Minh, who is said to be the father of Vietnam's founding

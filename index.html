About VNU University of Social Sciences and Humanities, Hanoi / ĐHQGHN Trường Đại học Khoa học Xã hội và Nhân văn, Hà Nội

Previous Next

Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU), formerly known as the University of Indochina in 1906. In 1945, on the basis of the University of Indochina, Vietnam National University was established and later renamed the University of Hanoi in 1956. In 1993, VNU was reorganized on the basis of amalgamating three leading universities in Hanoi, with the aim of establishing a high quality, multidisciplinary and multi-sectoral regional centre that gradually reaches international standards.
